Three Creative Ideas

Check out these professionally done creative ads on Pinterest: http://pin.it/s5VVI7p

The goal of the activities this week is to start brainstorming creative advertisement concepts for the Creative Ad project. Do the following:

  1. Take a look at the Creative Ad project requirements.
  2. Use the generator below to determine a product and target audience.
  3. Utilize some of the creativity activities completed earlier in the week to come up with ideas for advertising the chosen product.
  4. Try to focus on symbolic or non-literal concepts.
  5. Make quick sketches and write an explanation of your creative ideas.

Add & Subtract

You should have picked something to add and subtract from your daily routine this week. You will be reporting on this in the Three Creative Ideas Report. You will participate over and report on at least 5 days. See the Increase Creativity page for more information about this activity.

 

Three Creative Ideas Report

Once you have completed your 5-day altered daily routine and determined your three creative ideas, you will complete a Three Creative Ideas Report. This report will be what you submit for this assignment.

  1. Download this Three Creative Ideas Report template.
    • Click on the above Google Doc link.
    • Select File -> Download as -> Microsoft Word (.docx).
    • Open the downloaded file in Microsoft Word.
  2. Fill out the information requested.
  3. Save it as a PDF, and submit it in I-Learn.
