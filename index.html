Submission Countdown

Submissions need to be turned in by 12/6/2018

Step #1

Choose a competition to participate in. Know that you can always turn in multiple submissions, and you can participate in multiple competitions. A list of all the competitions for this semester can be found below.

Step #2

Make sure you follow all the requirements for the competition(s) you are working on. Document and share your progress on the BYU-I Visual Comm Society & Emphasis Facebook group. Join this group today: https://www.facebook.com/groups/visualcomm/

Step #3

You will complete a PDF case study for each competition submission. This is what you will be turning in as your submission for the competitions. Each case study will have different requirements. Be sure you have read, understand, and complete all requirements associated with the project and associated case study.

Step #4

Once completed, submit your completed case study PDF using this form.
